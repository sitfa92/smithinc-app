## What Are Digitals?
Digitals (also called "comp cards" or "digital photos") are simple, unretouched photographs used by agencies and clients to assess a model's look. They typically include:
- A front-facing headshot (natural lighting, minimal makeup)
- A full-body front photo
- A full-body side profile photo
- A 3/4 angle photo
Photos should be taken in fitted, neutral-colored clothing against a clean white or light background.

## Model Development Curriculum
Smith Inc's model development curriculum covers:
- **Posture & Movement** — runway technique, pivot turns, posing basics
- **Posing for Photography** — editorial vs. commercial, angles, expressions
- **Industry Knowledge** — understanding agencies, castings, go-sees, and bookings
- **Grooming & Presentation** — skincare routines, hair care, professional hygiene
- **Brand Building** — social media presence, personal branding for talent
- **Business of Modeling** — contracts, rates, rights, working with agencies

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does the review process take?**
A: Our team reviews submissions within 3–5 business days. You will be contacted by phone or email once your materials have been reviewed.

**Q: Do I need prior modeling experience?**
A: No. Smith Inc works with talent at all levels, including beginners. Our development program is designed to train and prepare new talent.

**Q: What age range does Smith Inc represent?**
A: Smith Inc works with talent aged 16 and older. Minors must have parental or guardian consent.

**Q: How do I submit my digitals?**
A: You will receive a secure upload link via email. Use that link to upload your photos. If you haven't received the link, WhatsApp us at +1 (773) 694-4567.

**Q: Can I submit from outside the United States?**
A: Yes. Smith Inc works with international talent. Please note that placement opportunities may vary by location.

**Q: What happens after my materials are reviewed?**
A: If your profile is a match, a Smith Inc representative will contact you to discuss the next steps, which may include an in-person or virtual consultation.

**Q: What is the difference between a model and a brand ambassador?**
A: Models primarily work on runway shows, photo shoots, and editorial campaigns. Brand ambassadors focus on promoting brands through events and social media content. Some talent do both.

**Q: How do I know if I received my digitals upload link?**
A: Check your email inbox and spam/junk folder. If you still don't see it, send a message to our WhatsApp at +1 (773) 694-4567.
